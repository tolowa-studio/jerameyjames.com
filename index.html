§ 01 — premise

the arc.

by — jj
// studio.raw · smith river · 2026.04
PHOTO 02/04

The real story is the bench. Tolowa Studio ships MOTION — the three-OS stack we install in small businesses to turn AI into repeatable throughput. Business OS for decision and judgment. Web OS for product delivery. GTM OS for distribution. Notion is the human brain, GitHub is the agent brain, GCP Secret Manager is the vault, Claude + n8n is the runtime. It's the operating system underneath every other venture on this page.

Konative is the intelligence layer for the $75B modular data center buildout — curated market intel, power-by-ISO data, and development-readiness brokerage for the principals, IDCs, and sovereign-wealth-backed funds actually deploying capital. SpokaneWire is the franchise-ready, AI-first hyperlocal — 347K Facebook reach, 67 live cameras, 118+ tracked incidents, replicable across ~300 U.S. cities. Modern Bees is the lifestyle play — a working small farm built as a clean D2C honey brand. More ventures go live as they're ready.

Tolowa Pacific is the base. Founded 2011 — tribal IT, carrier-grade network architecture, AMT-backed Ciena deals, broadband for sovereign nations. Earlier chapters: Gartner and Presidio consulting, then Compunet's CSP practice ($400 → $32M ARR in five years), then Arrow Electronics partner channel. The playbook never changed — sequence the underserved buyer, get inside the cap-ex cycle, ship the right architecture, compound relationships into deal flow.

The through-line is simple: systems that run without someone manually running them.
